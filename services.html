Our Services

  • Home
  • Services

At Sorelle Consults and Logistics Co. Ltd., we specialize in optimizing your operational needs with our expert services. From efficient shipping and regulatory management to precise financial reporting, our team ensures your business runs smoothly and adheres to all necessary standards, enhancing overall performance and compliance.

Our Range of Business-Centric Services include;

1. Freight Forwarding

We provide end-to-end freight forwarding solutions, managing every stage of the transport process. Our services in this include;

  • Cargo Handling: Efficiently managing the storage, loading, and unloading of your goods.
  • Route Optimization: Identifying and implementing the most cost-effective and time-efficient routes for transportation.
  • Documentation Management: Handling all necessary paperwork to ensure compliance and smooth transit.

2. Customs Clearance

Our customs clearance services are designed to simplify the process and ensure your goods move through customs smoothly;

  • Regulatory Compliance: Adhering to all relevant customs regulations and guidelines.
  • Documentation Preparation: Accurate preparation and submission of all required customs documents.
  • Issue Resolution: Proactively addressing and resolving any issues that may arise during the customs process.

3. Tax Computations

We offer precise tax computation services to help you meet your legal obligations;

  • VAT Computation: Calculating VAT for your business transactions accurately.
  • PAYE Management: Handling the computation and submission of employee tax withholdings.
  • NSSF Contributions: Managing contributions to the National Social Security Fund.
  • Income Tax Computation: Ensuring accurate calculation of income tax liabilities.

3. Return Filing

Our return filing services cover all necessary tax reporting requirements;

  • VAT Returns: Preparing and filing VAT returns in accordance with regulations.
  • PAYE Returns: Submitting PAYE returns to meet legal obligations.
  • NSSF Returns: Filing returns for NSSF contributions.
  • Income Tax Returns: Handling the submission of income tax returns to ensure compliance.
