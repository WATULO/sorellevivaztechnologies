Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Here are some frequently asked questions and their answers, providing valuable insights and guidance about us, our services and expertise.

1. How does customs clearance work?

Customs clearance is the process of ensuring that goods meet all regulatory requirements before they are allowed to enter or exit a country. We handle the preparation and submission of necessary documents, ensuring compliance with customs regulations and addressing any issues that arise.

2. What tax computations do you offer?

We provide tax computation services for VAT, PAYE, NSSF, and income tax. Our services ensure accurate calculations and compliance with Ugandan tax laws.

3. How can I ensure compliance with tax regulations?

Our team manages all aspects of tax computations and return filings to ensure that your business meets all legal requirements accurately and on time, helping you stay compliant with tax regulations.

4. What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of businesses, from small startups to large corporations, offering tailored solutions to meet their specific logistics and consulting needs.

5. How can I get started with your services?

You can get started by contacting us through our website or by phone. We’ll provide a consultation to discuss your needs and explain how our services can benefit your business.
